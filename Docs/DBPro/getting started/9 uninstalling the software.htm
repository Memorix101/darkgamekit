Uninstalling The Software

1. To remove the software from your computer, click the START button on the desktop and click the Control Panel.



2. Click the "Add or Remove Programs" item from the list, and scroll down until you locate Dark Basic Professional in the list of installed software.

3. Depending on your operating system this window will differ slightly. You will be able to highlight the Dark Basic Professional software item and remove it using a Remove button either located next to the item highlighted or elsewhere in the Add/Remove window.

4. Click the Remove button to run the software uninstaller. Follow the instructions given by the uninstaller to remove the software from your computer.
